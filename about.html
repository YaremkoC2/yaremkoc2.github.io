About Me

Hey, I'm currently working as a systems administrator for a small-medium company that works in oilfield fabrication. During the week I manage IT systems and dive into C# customizations for the Acumatica ERP platform. On weekends, I work a second job teaching coding to kids. I've always believed computer science should be a core subject in schools, so helping the next generation learn and get excited about tech is the coolest part of my week.

When I'm not coding or trouble shooting something at work, you're likely to find me playing games with my friends, watching basketball, or spending time with family. I also like working on hands on projects, especially in the embedded systems space. There's something about the merging of software and hardware that makes it extra fun.

This site is a place for me to share some of the projects I've worked on, tinker with ideas, and hopefully connect with others who share similar interests.
