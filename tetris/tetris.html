Controls & Rules

Controls

  • ← Move Left
  • → Move Right
  • ↓ Move Down
  • ↑ Rotate
  • Space Hold Piece

Scoring

  • Clear 1 Line: 100 x Level points
  • Clear 2 Lines: 300 x Level points
  • Clear 3 Lines: 500 x Level points
  • Clear 4 Lines (Tetris): 800 x Level points

Leveling

  • Level increases every 10 lines cleared
  • Each level increases game speed (faster piece drop)
  • Minimum speed is capped at level 10

Use the hold and next piece panels to strategize! Try to build up for big clears to maximize points.
